CrewAI AMP Showcase¶

Sector-tailored, hands-on AMP scenarios — each compiled from one source into a self-guided tutorial and an instructor-led lab. Source and demo runbooks live in the repository.

Self-guided tutorials¶

  • Cpg · Triage a Consumer Complaint with an AI Crew — and Catch the One That's a Recall — Tutorial — In ~30 minutes, no code, you build a two-agent consumer-complaint triage crew, paste in a raw complaint, and watch it classify and extract the details, judge severity and regulatory reportability, escalate the one that's a genuine safety event — and, the part that matters when a recall might be on the line, flag the missing critical info (like the lot code) instead of inventing it, then draft an empathetic consumer reply, all in an auditable trace, deployed in your own workspace.
  • Financial Services · Build a Fraud-Triage AI Crew in AMP — and Watch It Think — Tutorial — In ~30 minutes, from your browser and with no code, you build a two-agent fraud-triage crew, run it on a transaction you choose, watch both agents reason step-by-step in a live trace, and deploy it as a real service in your own workspace.
  • Insurance · Triage an Insurance Claim with an AI Crew — and Watch It Refuse to Guess — Tutorial — In ~30 minutes, no code, you build a two-agent first-notice-of-loss (FNOL) triage crew, paste in a raw claim report, and watch it extract a structured claim with per-field confidence, flag every missing field and fraud indicator, recommend a routing decision — and, the part that matters when money and regulators are involved, leave the fields it can't support blank instead of inventing them, all in an auditable trace, deployed in your own workspace.
  • Pharma · Triage a Drug-Safety Report with an AI Crew — and Trust What It Doesn't Know — Tutorial — In ~30 minutes, no code, you build a two-agent pharmacovigilance intake crew, run it on an adverse-event report you paste, and watch it extract a structured safety case, assess seriousness, and — the part that matters in a regulated domain — flag every field it isn't sure of instead of fabricating it, all in an auditable trace, deployed in your own workspace.
  • Sales · Qualify an Inbound Lead with an AI Crew — Scored, Routed, and Honest About Guesses — Tutorial — In ~30 minutes, no code, you build a two-agent inbound-lead qualification crew, paste in a raw lead record, and watch it extract firmographics with per-field confidence, score ICP fit 0–100, and — the part that keeps your CRM clean — flag the fields it can't verify (and refuse to invent a website or a headcount) instead of guessing, then hand your rep a routing decision and a ready-to-use outreach angle, all in an auditable trace, deployed in your own workspace.
